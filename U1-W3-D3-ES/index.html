Genoa Cricket and Football Club

THE OLDEST ITALIAN FOOTBALL CLUB

Here's a time line of Genoa Cricket and Football Club:

Founded in 1893, it is the longest-running of the Italian clubs still in business as well as one of the most noble, boasting in its roll of honor 9 league titles won between 1898 - the first ever in the history of Italian football - and 1923-1924: this series of successes earned Genoa the definition of the first great national football, a role exercised until the pre-war period, and ensured that for its first sixty years it established itself as the most successful Italian team, alone or shared, before being overtaken by Juventus at the end of the second post-war period.

  • September 7, 1893 - Foundation of the Genoa Cricket and Athletic Club
  • 1898 - Champion of Italy (1st title)
  • 1899 - Champion of Italy (2nd title)
  • 1900 - Champion of Italy (3rd title)
  • 1902 - Champion of Italy (4th title).
  • 1903 - Champion of Italy (5th title).
  • 1904 - Champion of Italy (6th title).
  • 1915 - Champion of Italy (7th title).
  • 1923 - Champion of Italy (8th title).
  • 1924 - Champion of Italy (9th title).
  • 1937 - Winner of the Italian Cup (1st title).
  • 1962 - Cup of the Alps (1st title).
  • 1964 - Cup of the Alps (2nd title)
